About

Hi I'm Betsabé, my work combines web development, visual design, and user experience.

Graduated as an Architect, and having worked for several years in the field and in different countries, in 2020 I decided to take a break in my career to focus on an area that has always interested me, web development.

I am interested in frontend development and the design of functional, clean and user friendly interfaces

That is why I studied web development with OpenClassrooms in France, where I had the opportunity to carry out challenging projects, wich has helped me to increase my skills. In parallel I studied a Master's degree in Experience Design thanks to Shifta by Elisava, where I learned to have a global vision in the development of digital projects, and to understand the importance of the user as the center of product.

My main motivation is to continue developing my skills in the frontend area, and interaction design.
